Shawn Peter Raul Mendes is a Canadian singer and songwriter. The amount of followers he acquired started to increase rapidly as a result of posting covers to the app known as Vine. Shawn caught the eyes of Island Records who ended up signing him in 2014. He is the youngest artist to make his own album in the top 25 with a debut song found on Billboard Hot 100, debuting at number 24 for the week ending July 12, 2014, at 15 years old. By 2017, he was selling out Madison Square Garden in only 4 minutes and had two amazing albums. The albums featured number 1 songs like "Mercy", "Treat you Better", and "There's Nothing Holding me Back". Mendes released his first fragrance, "Shawn Signature" in late 2017 and came out with the second one in late 2018. Shawn's most recent releases include "In my blood" and "Lost in Japan" which are popular at the moment.
